About Our Platform

Combating Digital Misinformation

VeritasAI is a Final Year Project built to help people identify fake news using advanced AI technology.

To give everyone a simple, reliable way to check if news is real — so people can stay informed and avoid being misled.
Challenge
Information Overload

There is so much news online that it is hard to tell what is real and what is fabricated.

Solution
AI-Powered Verification

Our tool uses machine learning to analyze language patterns, sources, and credibility signals.

Technology
